## What is AI-Powered Design?

AI-powered design represents the intersection of artificial intelligence and creative processes. It encompasses tools, techniques, and workflows that use machine learning and AI algorithms to assist, enhance, or automate various aspects of design work.

## Key Topics

- **Image Generation**: Creating visuals with AI models like DALL-E, Midjourney, and Stable Diffusion
- **Typography**: AI-assisted font selection and text layout optimization
- **Color Theory**: Intelligent color palette generation and harmony
- **Layout Design**: Automated and AI-guided layout composition
- **Brand Identity**: AI tools for logo design and brand development
- **User Experience**: AI-enhanced UX research and interface design
